HIRA PRASAD WELFARE FOUNDATION
ANNUAL REPORT 2024

================================================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Hira Prasad Welfare Foundation (HPWF) is proud to present our Annual Report 
for 2024, showcasing the impact we've made in Samastipur, Bihar, and the lives 
we've touched through our dedicated programs.

================================================================================

MISSION STATEMENT

Empowering Lives, Enriching Futures

We are committed to uplifting vulnerable communities, particularly the elderly, 
children, and women, through comprehensive welfare programs that promote dignity, 
education, and empowerment.

================================================================================

2024 HIGHLIGHTS

• 500+ Seniors Supported through our Elderly Care Program
• 1,200+ Children Educated with scholarships and supplies
• 300+ Women Empowered through vocational training
• 50+ Active Programs running across Samastipur

PROGRAM ACHIEVEMENTS

ELDERLY CARE PROGRAM
- Established fully-equipped old age home
- Provided free medical checkups to 200+ residents
- Organized recreational activities and yoga sessions
- Ensured dignified living for senior citizens

CHILDREN'S WELFARE PROGRAM
- Distributed scholarships to 1,200+ underprivileged children
- Provided free uniforms and educational supplies
- Achieved 95% pass rate in annual exams
- 40% students achieved distinction

WOMEN'S EMPOWERMENT PROGRAM
- Trained 300+ women in vocational skills
- Facilitated 50+ women to start their own businesses
- Provided microfinance support
- Conducted awareness programs on women's rights

COMMUNITY SUPPORT PROGRAM
- Organized health camps in 15 villages
- Distributed hygiene kits to 500+ families
- Conducted awareness drives on sanitation and health
- Supported local community development initiatives
