Hira Prasad Welfare Foundation was founded by Akshita Singh with a vision to uplift the most vulnerable members of our society. Growing up in Samastipur, Bihar, Akshita witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by elderly citizens left without care, children deprived of education, and women struggling for basic opportunities.

What started as a personal calling has grown into a comprehensive welfare organization dedicated to creating lasting social change. Named in honor of values that emphasize service and compassion, our foundation operates on the principle that every individual‚Äîregardless of age, gender, or background‚Äîdeserves to live a dignified and fulfilling life.

Today, we work across Samastipur and neighboring regions, providing integrated support through our three core programs: elderly care, children's welfare, and women's empowerment. Our approach is rooted in respect, personalized attention, and sustainable community development.

### Challenges We Address

**Heading:** The Problems We're Solving

**Challenge 1: Lack of Elderly Care**
```
Many senior citizens in rural areas face neglect and lack proper care, leading to isolation and poor quality of life. Traditional family structures are changing, leaving the elderly without support systems.
```

**Challenge 2: Limited Access to Education**
```
Underprivileged children often lack access to quality education due to financial constraints, inadequate infrastructure, and social barriers, hindering their future opportunities and perpetuating cycles of poverty.
```

**Challenge 3: Health Inequality**
```
Marginalized communities, especially children, suffer from inadequate healthcare services and nutrition. Preventable diseases and malnutrition affect development and quality of life.
```

**Challenge 4: Gender Inequality**
```
Women in rural areas face barriers in education, employment, and social rights. Lack of skills and resources limits their empowerment and independence, affecting entire families.
```

### Program Overview

**Section Heading:**
```
Honoring Our Seniors with Dignity and Care
```

**Content:**
```
At HPWF, we believe that every senior citizen deserves to live their golden years with dignity, comfort, and companionship. Our Elderly Care Program provides a safe, respectful environment where seniors receive comprehensive care that addresses their physical, emotional, and social needs.

Many elderly individuals in rural Bihar face neglect, isolation, and lack of proper medical attention. Our old age home and care services fill this critical gap, offering a home where seniors are treated with the love and respect they deserve.
```

---

### What We Offer

**Heading:** Our Services

**Service 1: Old Age Home**
```
A safe, comfortable living environment designed specifically for senior citizens. Our facility provides private and shared accommodations with all necessary amenities, creating a home away from home.
```

**Service 2: Medical Care & Support**
```
Regular health check-ups, medication management, and access to medical professionals. We coordinate with local healthcare providers to ensure timely medical attention.
```

**Service 3: Nutritious Meals**
```
Balanced, nutritious meals prepared with consideration for dietary needs and preferences. Our kitchen staff ensures seniors receive proper nutrition for optimal health.
```

**Service 4: Recreational Activities**
```
Yoga sessions, cultural programs, games, and social gatherings keep our seniors engaged, active, and connected with their peers.
```

**Service 5: Emotional Well-being**
```
Regular counseling, spiritual support, and companionship programs address the emotional and psychological needs of our residents.
```

**Service 6: Family Engagement**
```
We encourage family visits and maintain open communication with relatives, ensuring seniors stay connected with their loved ones.
```

---

### Who Benefits

**Heading:** Who We Serve

**Content:**
```
Our Elderly Care Program serves:

- Senior citizens without family support or those whose families cannot provide adequate care
- Elderly individuals living in isolation with no social connections
- Seniors requiring medical attention and daily living assistance
- Those seeking companionship and a supportive community in their later years
- Low-income elderly individuals who cannot afford private care facilities

We welcome seniors regardless of caste, religion, or social background. Our only criteria is the need for care and our ability to provide it.
```
